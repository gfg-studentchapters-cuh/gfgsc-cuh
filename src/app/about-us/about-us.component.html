about us

what makes us special?

It's been a pleasure for Central University of Haryana to be in collaboration with the very renowned 'Geeks for Geeks'. GFG is portal for coders and programmers to develop the technical aspect around the world. As a student's chapter of GFG we are privileged to bring this opportunity to CUH. It’s been a hustle to be a programmer in new age.

Concluding it to the fact that our student’s chapter of CUH and GFG has main motive to enhance the ideas and concepts of programmers at our university with various events, programs and competitions.



what defines our vision?

In GeeksforGeeks student Chapter CUH , Our focus would be on:-

Uplifting techinical skills and improving coding skills through workshops,hackathons,webinars.
Creating oppportunities through which students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build effective and optimized solutions for local businesses and their community.
Updating students with valuable content and opportunities to apply for/tech opportuities,internships and jobs
